How to Play Golf



GOAL

The goal is to have the lowest score after 9 holes.



SCORING

Card Points
Number cards (3-10) Face value
Aces 1 point
2s -2 points
Kings 0 points
Queens/Jacks 10 points
Matching Pairs
(Same rank in column)		 0 points
(Values not added)


GAMEPLAY

At the begging of the game, click on two cards to flip over.

On your turn, draw from the deck or discard pile by clicking on the pile. Swap the drawn card with one of your 6 cards or discard it if it came from the deck by clicking on the discard pile or the card to replace.

When one player flips all their cards, everyone else gets one last turn.
